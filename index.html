Frequently Asked Questions

Are you licensed to fly drones commercially?

Yes. We hold an FAA Part 107 Remote Pilot Certificate and follow all federal and local airspace regulations.

What happens if the weather is bad?

Safety comes first. We monitor forecasts closely and will reschedule at no extra charge if wind, rain, or low visibility prevents a safe flight.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend 2–4 weeks for events, especially weekends and holidays. Short-notice bookings are possible based on availability.

Can you fly indoors?

Indoor flight is limited to large venues with proper safety clearance. Tell us about your space and we'll assess feasibility.

What deliverables will I receive?

Edited digital photos and video files delivered through a private online gallery. RAW files available by request.
